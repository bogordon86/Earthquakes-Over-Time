GeoJSON API endpoints:
Here is the endpoint for the all week data (roughly less than 2000 earthquakes):
"https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/all_week.geojson"

Here is the endpoint for the past day data (less than 200 earthquakes):
"https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/all_day.geojson"

If you really wanted to go big or go home you could use the all month dataset (over 10,000 earthquakes):
"https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/all_month.geojson"
